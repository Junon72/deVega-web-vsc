ABOUT

I HAVE ALWAYS BEEN INTERESTED IN PROCESSES AND IN THE ACT OF CREATION ITSELF.

MY PASSION FOR MUSIC (AND MUSIC MAKING) HAS LED ME TO EXPLORING THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN MUSIC PRODUCTION/COMPOSITION AND CHOREOGRAPHY/MOVEMENT GENERATION. I AM LOOKING FOR A PLAYFUL BALANCE ENCOMPASSING THE FREEDOM AND THE DYNAMICS OF A CONCERT WITH THE (HIDDEN) PRECISION OF A CHOREOGRAPHED PIECE.

I CONSIDER MY WORK TO BE ATMOSPHERIC AND IMMERSIVE. I CREATE PHYSICAL, VISUAL AND AURAL LANDSCAPES, LIKE A COLLECTION OF MOODS THAT REFLECT CURRENT SOCIO-POLITICAL ISSUES WITH SOME REFERENCES TO ART AND/OR POP CULTURE ADDED TO THE MIX.

IN PREPARATION FOR MY UPCOMING WORK (STILL UNTITLED), AND CONTINUING THE PATH OPENED BY MY LATEST PIECE CHOREOPOP, I AM DEVELOPING A CREATIVE AND SCORING METHOD (CALLED MUSICOGRAPHY) THAT WILL SERVE AS THE GENERATOR FOR PHYSICAL/AURAL OUTCOMES.

IN 2012/13 I PARTICIPATED IN SPAZIO, A EUROPEAN PROGRAMME FOR DANCE MAKERS. CURRENTLY I AM A SUPPORTED ARTIST AT ICK AND DANSMAKERS, BOTH LOCATED IN AMSTERDAM.
