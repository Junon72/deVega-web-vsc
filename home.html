About

I have alvays been interested in processes and in the act of creation itself.

My passion for music (and music making) has let me to exploring the relationship between music production/composition and choreography/movement generation. I am looking for a palyful balanced encompassing the freedom and the dynamics of a concert with the (hidden) precision of a choreographed piece.

I consider my work to be atmospheric and immersive. I create physical, visual and aural landscapes, like a collection of moods that replicate current socio-political issues with some references to art and /or pop clture addd to the mix.

In prepareation for my upcoming work (still untitled), and counting the path opened by my latest piece 'Choreopop', I am developing a creative and scoring method (called 'Musicography') that will serve as a generator for physical/aural outcomes.

In 2012/13 I participated in Spazio, a European programme for dance makers. Currently I am supported artist at ICK and Dansmakers, both located in Amterdam.

As a dancer I have worked with companies and choreographers such as ICK, Galili Dance, Iceland Dance Company, Metros, Stephen Shropshire, André Gingras, Sylvain Emard and Václav Kuneš amongst others. I workded as well as a dance teacher/coach, giving workshops or imparting classes for instituions like Fontys, AHK, Artez, Codarts, Norrdans, ICK, HJS, Akbank Sanat (Turkey), NSCD (Leeds), CAD (Seville) etc. to name but a few.

My work has toured accross The Netherlands and has been among others programmed in theaters like Frascati, Meervaart, Bellevue, Korzo, NWE Vorst, Grand Theater Groningen, Parktheater. I also have had a priviledge to to show my work at Dutch festivals like Mundial, Nazomerfestival, Boulevard Festival and The Nederlandse Dansdagen as an example. Internationally, I had the change to show in Istanbul and premiere a work in the U.K.

I was born in the Canry Islands (Spain) and I am currently based in Amsterdam (The Netherlands)

Dance your way to the future.
